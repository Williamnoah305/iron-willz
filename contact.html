Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my artwork?

Go to the Upload page, drag & drop your files or browse to select them. Fill in the title, description, category, and tags, then click 'Upload Now'. Your work will appear in the gallery immediately!

What file formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG for images; WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG for audio; and MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM for videos. Maximum file size is 500MB per upload.

Is IRON WILLZ free to use?

Yes! IRON WILLZ is completely free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations. Upload as much as you want!

Can I delete my uploads?

Yes, you can delete any of your uploads from the Recent Uploads section on the Upload page. Simply click the delete button next to the item.

Where are my files stored?

Your files are stored in your browser's local storage. They remain on your device and are accessible whenever you open IRON WILLZ.

How can I filter content in the gallery?

Visit the Gallery page and use the filter buttons to view All content, Images only, Music only, or Videos only. You can also search using tags.
